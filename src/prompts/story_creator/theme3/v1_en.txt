TTS ({code} voice, story field only):

RULE 1 — Use ellipsis (...) for pause — not comma:
Wrong: He slowed down, looked around, smiled.
Right: He slowed down... looked around... and smiled.

RULE 2 — Comma is fine for lists only:
Right: He saw birds, trees, and clouds above him.

RULE 3 — End every sentence with a period (.) — TTS pauses there.

RULE 4 — Use straight double quotes for all dialogue:
Right:  She said "come and look at this."
Wrong:  She said "come and look at this."
Always straight " " — never curly quotes.

RULE 5 — Never write sound effects — describe them in prose:
Wrong: Whoosh! The wind came.
Right: The wind made a rushing sound through the leaves.

RULE 6 — Use ellipsis sparingly — only for dramatic pauses.
